The Tent

Luxury for the whole group

Perched atop its own private hilltop with panoramic 360° views across the Waitomo countryside, the Pōhutukawa Tent is our flagship experience. Built to sleep up to eight guests across four queen bedrooms, it's perfect for families, friend groups, milestone birthdays, and memorable celebrations.

Inside, a double-sided fireplace anchors the open-plan living space. Underfloor heating, a full indoor kitchen with dishwasher, 2.5 bathrooms, and insulated double-glazed windows mean you're as comfortable in winter as in summer. Outside, a cedar hot tub pre-heated to 38°C, wood-fired pizza oven, Weber BBQ and firepit complete the picture. Everything runs seamlessly, with all the comforts of home.

Powered by Nature

State-of-the-art off-grid power

The Pōhutukawa Tent runs entirely on renewable energy via a purpose-built system — but you would never know it. Every appliance, the hot tub, underfloor heating and hot water all run seamlessly, just as they would at home.

Getting There

Most two-wheel-drive vehicles can access the Pōhutukawa Tent. Parking is available close to the tent with no significant uphill walk required.

State-of-the-Art Off-Grid Power

A purpose-built system harnesses solar and renewable energy to run every appliance, the hot tub, underfloor heating and hot water without compromise. Most guests never even realise it.

Connectivity

WiFi, good mobile and 4G coverage from the hilltop. The views, however, are a compelling reason to put the phone away entirely.

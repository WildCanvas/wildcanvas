About Wild Canvas

Nestled in the rolling hills of Waitomo, Wild Canvas offers luxury glamping escapes designed for connection — with nature, loved ones, and the quiet moments that matter most. Two safari style tents, the Kōwhai and Pōhutukawa, combine comfort, craftsmanship and wilderness into an unforgettable New Zealand experience.

Recent Features

Wild Canvas in print and online

Kia Ora Magazine

September 2025

Wild Canvas featured in Air New Zealand's inflight magazine

Wild Canvas was featured in Kia Ora, Air New Zealand's inflight magazine, as part of a travel story on the Waitomo region. The feature highlighted our hilltop sanctuary as the perfect base for exploring the area's caves, waterfalls and lush countryside.

Air New Zealand

2024

Part of Air New Zealand's Fall in Love with New Zealand campaign

Wild Canvas is proud to be part of Air New Zealand's romantic travel guide celebrating meaningful getaways across Aotearoa. Our Waitomo campfire experience was featured as the perfect way for couples to share stories under the stars, alongside our private hot tubs and panoramic hilltop views.

Life & Leisure Magazine

January 2024

A full feature in one of New Zealand's leading lifestyle publications

Life and Leisure captured the Wild Canvas story beautifully — from a bold move away from Auckland city life to creating a peaceful sanctuary on a family farm in Waitomo. Written by Sarah Templeton, photographed by Tessa Chrisp and Lisa Sun, the feature tells a story of serendipity, hard work, and heart.

Availability

Massages are subject to availability. We recommend booking as early as possible to avoid missing out, particularly over weekends and peak periods.

Groups of Four or More

For bookings of four or more massages, we arrange additional therapists so everyone can be seen without a long wait. Let us know your group size when booking.

Pregnancy Massage

Pregnancy massage is available as part of the back, neck and shoulder option. Please mention this when booking so we can arrange the right therapist.

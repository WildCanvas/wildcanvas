Food & Catering

Great food, without the effort

All catering is prepared fresh and waiting at your tent on arrival. Fire up the pizza oven, open the platter, or let us take care of dinner entirely. At least one week's notice required.

Kōwhai Tent — Included with every stay

  • 4 organic eggs and 750ml organic milk (Puhoi Valley)
  • Butter and a loaf of fresh homemade bread
  • S'mores kit: marshmallows and biscuits
  • English Breakfast tea, plunger coffee, instant coffee and sugar
  • Condiments: salt, pepper, oil and flour
  • Outdoor kitchen: wood-fired pizza oven, Weber BBQ and gas hob
  • Chilly bin stocked with ice packs for fresh food

Pōhutukawa Tent — Included with every stay

  • Bottle of New Zealand wine
  • Milk, yoghurt, butter, eggs and cereal
  • S'mores kit: marshmallows and biscuits
  • English Breakfast tea, plunger coffee and sugar
  • Condiments: salt, pepper and oil
  • Full indoor kitchen: oven, induction cooktop, microwave, fridge, freezer and dishwasher
  • Outdoor: Morsø wood-fired pizza oven and Weber BBQ

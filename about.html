Our Story

Born from a farm,
built with love

Wild Canvas is a family-run luxury glamping experience on a 700-hectare, third-generation farm in the heart of Waitomo. Two private hilltop tents. One simple belief: that the best getaways happen when luxury meets nature.

The Idea

Luxury that belongs outdoors

We have always believed there is something special about sleeping under canvas. A feeling that connects you to the land in a way no building can. From inside a tent, you hear the soft rustle of the wind, the distant call of birds at dawn, the gentle hum of farm life below.

Glamping, for us, is about reconnecting to nature, to each other, and to yourself, while still enjoying all the comforts that make a stay truly restorative.

Our tents sit on the farm's most spectacular ridgelines, completely private and surrounded by sweeping views. Here, nature takes centre stage: watch the sun dip behind the hills, the stars appear one by one, and the early morning mist roll through the valleys.

Meet Your Hosts

Emily & Benjamin

Proud hosts of Wild Canvas and the family behind everything you experience here. Between us, we bring engineering, design, farming, and a deep love of this land.

Benjamin Scott

Co-Founder, Farmer

Ben grew up on this farm and returned in 2016 to help run the family farm after a career in civil engineering. He completed his MBA in 2022, exploring regenerative farming and sustainable business practices, ideas that continue to shape Wild Canvas from energy-efficient design to careful native planting across the farm.

Emily Scott

Co-Founder, Creative Director

Emily grew up in Auckland and, after visiting the farm and seeing its beauty, dreamed of sharing it with others. Originally from South Africa, it felt fitting that the first build was a safari tent — a nod to her birthplace and her love of design. She leads the creative vision behind Wild Canvas, from the interiors to the guest experience.

How We Got Here

The Wild Canvas journey

2016

Back to the land

Ben returns to help run the family farm full time, taking on the third-generation property in Waitomo and beginning to think about what else the land could offer.

2017

The first tent opens

Wild Canvas opens with its first luxury safari tent. Inspired by Emily's South African roots and a shared love of design and nature, it quickly becomes one of New Zealand's most loved glamping experiences.

2022

Airbnb Best Design Stay Award

Wild Canvas receives the Airbnb Best Design Stay Award, recognising the thoughtful design and guest experience behind the Kōwhai Tent.

2023

The Pōhutukawa Tent opens

After two years of meticulous planning, engineering, and building, the Pōhutukawa Tent opens its doors. Four bedrooms, cedar hot tub, full kitchen, and a state-of-the-art sustainable power system.

2024

5-Star Qualmark Gold Award

Wild Canvas achieves New Zealand's first 5-Star Qualmark Glamping rating, along with the prestigious Gold Sustainable Tourism Business Award.

2025

The new Kōwhai Tent

The Kōwhai Tent is completely redesigned and relaunched with an indoor fireplace, polished floors, twin claw foot baths and a new native landscape design. A fresh chapter for our most loved couples' retreat.

The Farm

700 hectares of Waitomo

Wild Canvas sits on a working farm, home to both beef and dairy herds, with sheep grazing the paddocks around the Pōhutukawa Tent. Mornings begin with birdsong echoing through the valleys.

Guests witness real farm life while enjoying complete privacy and comfort. From your hilltop, take in sweeping views of the farmland, breathe fresh country air, and feel immersed in nature, with every luxury just a few steps away.

AWARDS & RECOGNITION

November 2025

New Zealand's First and Only 5-Star Glamping Experience

When we first opened Wild Canvas, our dream was simple: to share the beauty of New Zealand by creating a place where people could slow down, reconnect, and truly appreciate the landscape around them.

We wanted to build a space where guests could feel deeply connected to nature while still being pampered with thoughtful design, warmth, and comfort. A place where precious memories with loved ones could be made — because those moments are ultimately what matter most.

Earlier this year, we were honoured to have that vision recognised with one of New Zealand's highest tourism accolades: Qualmark 5-Star Glamping and Qualmark Gold Sustainable Tourism Business Award. And today, almost a full year later, we are still proudly New Zealand's first and only 5-star glamping destination.

What makes a glamping site 5-star?

Qualmark's criteria for glamping use the same rigorous standards applied to top lodges and premium hospitality providers. That means everything — from design and construction to safety, sustainability, guest experience, and cleanliness — must meet luxury-level expectations.

For us, this includes two architecturally designed safari tents perched on private hilltops, lodge-style interiors with boutique amenities, private outdoor baths, hot tubs and panoramic views, Gold standard sustainability practices, meticulous care and hosting, and a level of privacy and tranquillity that's hard to find anywhere else. Every detail is intentional. Every stay should feel effortless, restorative, and unforgettable.

Why this award matters

There are many beautiful glamping sites across New Zealand, but to date, no others have reached 5-star glamping status through Qualmark's assessment programme. The star rating is not about luxury alone — it's about continual excellence, sustainability, and the systems behind the scenes that ensure guests have an exceptional experience every time.

Being the only operator to achieve this in New Zealand is something we are incredibly proud of.

Thank you

To everyone who has stayed with us, shared our story, supported us through upgrades and rebuilds, or simply followed along — thank you. You've helped shape Wild Canvas into what it is today. We look forward to welcoming many more guests to experience New Zealand's only 5-star glamping lodge in the heart of Waitomo.

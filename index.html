Waitomo, New Zealand

Luxury that belongs in nature

Nestled in the North Island's Waitomo region, Wild Canvas was born from a simple belief: luxury and wildness belong together. Two canvas tents, two hilltops, over a kilometre apart. Each one dressed in quality linens, thoughtful décor, and the kind of quiet you only find somewhere genuinely remote.

Wake to birdsong. Soak in an outdoor bath as mist rolls across the valley. Gather at the campfire as stars appear. No schedules, no interruptions, no one else for miles.

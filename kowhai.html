The Tent

Slow living, beautifully done

Thoughtfully redesigned in 2025, the Kōwhai Tent captures the essence of slow living. Inside, you'll find an indoor fireplace, polished floors and soft layers of natural linen. The design is cosy yet refined, with moody hues, tactile materials, and framed hilltop views through glass doors that open to the deck. The tent is fully insulated with double-glazed glass throughout, keeping you cosy in every season.

Outside, a native landscape design invites you to unwind. Soak in the twin claw foot baths overlooking the valley, share stories by the firepit, or cook under the stars in your private outdoor kitchen.

Getting There

The track requires a 4WD or AWD

The unsealed track up to the Kōwhai Tent is steep — a 4WD or AWD vehicle is required. If you don't have one, no problem at all. Guests are welcome to borrow our Suzuki Jimny free of charge. Just let us know ahead of your arrival and we will have it ready and waiting.

From the carpark it is a short 120m walk uphill to the tent, so pack light and wear comfortable shoes.

Your Stay

How your time together could look

A private hilltop, just the two of you. Good food, fresh air, outdoor baths, and fires beneath the stars.

Arrival

Arrive & settle in

Drive the Jimny up the hill, step onto your private hilltop, and feel the world drop away. Open a platter, pour a drink, and take in the Waitomo views from the deck or the lawn.

Afternoon

Slow down

Add an in-tent massage or sink into the twin outdoor baths as the afternoon softens.

Evening

Make dinner together

Light the pizza oven and cook under the open sky. The outdoor kitchen is fully equipped — dinner at your own pace, with one of the best views in Waitomo as your backdrop.

Night

Settle into the night

Toast marshmallows over the firepit, let the valley go quiet around you, and stay out under the stars longer than intended. Just the two of you, completely undisturbed.

Late Night

End the night outside

Watch a movie under the open sky on the outdoor projector, bean bags, lantern lit. Stay up a little later than planned — you've earned it.

Morning

Wake slowly

Coffee, breakfast, birdsong. Check-out is 11am — so there's genuinely no rush. Pull on the robes, watch the mist lift off the Waitomo valley, and let the morning come to you.

Off-Grid

The Kōwhai Tent is fully off-grid. Water is gas-heated and power packs are supplied for small devices. Solar lighting throughout.

Getting There

A short walk uphill from the carpark (approx. 120m). The track is steep, so pack light. A 4WD or AWD is required. Guests are welcome to borrow our Suzuki Jimny free of charge if needed.

Connectivity

There's excellent mobile and 4G coverage from the hilltop. We recommend leaving the phone in your bag, the views are better.

Explore Waitomo

From the world famous Glowworm Caves to hidden bush walks, ziplines, and Lord of the Rings film locations — Waitomo and the surrounding region is packed with unforgettable experiences. Wild Canvas is your perfect base.

Caves & Adventures

Underground wonders

Waitomo sits on a vast limestone karst landscape, making it one of the best places in the world for cave exploration and underground adventure.

Must Do 13 min drive

Waitomo Glowworm Caves

One of New Zealand's most popular attractions. Take a guided boat tour through the spectacular underground cathedral cave, lit from above by thousands of tiny glowworms. Magical for all ages.

Book a tour →
Adventure 13 min drive

Lost World Adventures

Described by Lonely Planet as "one of the best things you can do in this country." Rappel 100 metres into a stunning underground cave system. Not for the faint-hearted, but absolutely unforgettable.

Find out more →
Family 13 min drive

Troll Cave

A magical 1.5-hour family adventure through Tom the Troll's underground lair. Kids use their stealth and survival instincts to complete their mission. Imaginative and thrilling for younger adventurers.

Find out more →

Nature Walks

Walks and waterfalls

The Waitomo region is laced with beautiful DOC walking tracks through native bush, over suspension bridges, and past spectacular limestone formations.

Easy Walk 19 min drive

Ruakuri Walk

A beautiful loop through native New Zealand bush, limestone formations, and past a stunning waterfall. Highlights include the impressive Mangapohue Natural Bridge limestone archway and native birds including tui and kererū.

DOC track info →
Moderate Walk 50 min drive

Marokopa Falls Walk

A scenic walk through beautiful forested areas, over a suspension bridge, to the base of the 35-metre Marokopa Falls. Several viewing platforms make it a wonderful spot for photos. Well worth the drive.

DOC track info →
Wildlife 15 min drive

The Kiwi House

One of the few places to see the iconic kiwi in a natural nocturnal environment. The park is also home to kea, kākā, and tuatara. A wonderful stop for families and anyone keen to learn about New Zealand's native species.

Visit the Kiwi House →

Activities

Ziplines and e-bikes

For those who want a bit more action alongside the scenery, Waitomo has it covered.

Adventure 14 min drive

Waitomo Caves Zipline

Soar through native trees and limestone bluffs on 10 ziplines, the longest spanning 280m at speeds up to 80km/hr. A two-hour adventure incorporating local Maori history. Great for families and groups.

Book a session →
Cycling 13 min drive

Waitomo Ebikes

Explore New Zealand's stunning landscapes by e-bike, from the Waitomo Caves to the rugged West Coast. Self-guided or expert-led rides ranging from one hour to a full day on scenic gravel and sealed roads.

Explore rides →

A Little Further Afield

Beyond Waitomo

Wild Canvas makes a great base for exploring the wider North Island. These two are well worth the day trip.

1 hr 13 min drive

Hobbiton Movie Set

Wander through the lush pastures of the Shire, explore the iconic hobbit holes, and enjoy a drink at the famous Green Dragon Inn. Whether you are a Lord of the Rings devotee or simply love extraordinary landscapes, this is worth every minute of the drive.

Book Hobbiton tickets →

44 min drive

Hairy Feet: Hobbit Film Location

A guided tour through dramatic limestone cliffs and lush farmland used as filming locations in The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey. Step into Middle-earth, hear behind-the-scenes stories, and soak in the scenery. A must for film lovers and anyone wanting to experience New Zealand's cinematic landscapes.
